## Frank Teruel

#### Adjunct Lecturer, Accounting

* * *

Frank Teruel is an experienced executive with significant domain knowledge in
sales, finance, and operations.  He is currently the CFO of ThreatMetrix Inc.,
the global leader in Digital Identity solutions and Cyber Threat prevention.
As COO of Lightpost Holdings, a global publishing, licensing, and media
company, Teruel held global P/L responsibility and directed all group
operating activities.  He also served as the President of the Thomas Kinkade
Foundation.  Prior to joining Lightpost, Teruel served as the CFO and VP of
World Wide Services and Support for Vormetric, an enterprise data security and
encryption company.  Prior to joining Vormetric, Teruel served as Vice
President of Finance for Emagia Corporation, an enterprise software company
focused on working capital optimization solutions.  Prior to Emagia, he served
as CFO for TelePost, Inc., an international provider of conferencing and
collaborative solutions. As Managing Director of Montgomery Financial
Management, Teruel was responsible for all aspects of the Northern and
Southern California practices. He also brings experience from SyQuest, Inc
(Nasdaq: SYQTQ), where he held senior financial and sales management
positions, and PricewaterhouseCoopers.

Teruel is an adjunct professor at Santa Clara University’s Leavey School of
Business. He frequently speaks on the Cyber Security imperatives and internet
fraud prevention and has contributed articles on data security.  He is the
founder of AquaMunda, an organization dedicated to clean water solutions for
the developing world.  Teruel holds an MBA from Liberty University, a BA
degree in Business Administration, and is a CPA.
