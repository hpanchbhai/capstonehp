  | **MS Business Analytics** | **MS Financial Analytics** | **MS Information
Systems**  
---|---|---|---  
**Course Competitive Advantages** |

Deep connection to Silicon Valley

|

Our top ranked Finance program is designed jointly by world-renowned faculty
and industry experts to prepare future finance leaders to succeed in Silicon
Valley and beyond

|

Deep skills in information technology, data science, and cloud computing
combined with business skills for building, leading, and representing teams  
  
**Course Highlights** |  Machine Learning,Reinforcement Learning, DeepLearning
| Financial Forecasting & Analysis, Corporate Finance, Investments, Analytics
of Finance, Fin Tech, Entrepreneurial Finance | Machine Learning,  Artificial
Intelligence, Cloud Computing, Big Data Modeling, Enterprise Systems  
**Goals, Outcomes and Skill sets** |  Excellent balance of business and data
science. Learn to ask the right questions. | Solid finance foundation combined
with data analytics skills | Development, Design, and Analytics Foundations  
Cloud Architecture and Enterprise Systems Design  
Data Science Skills  
**Industry Job Titles** |  Business Analyst, Data Scientist, Marketing
Analyst, Finance Analyst  | Financial Manager, Business Manager, Business
Analyst, Financial Analyst, Financial Advisor | Technical Program Manager,
Cloud engineer, Information technology manager, Information systems analyst,
Software developer, Business consultant  
**Companies where you will find SCU alumni** |  Google, Apple, EY, Cisco,
Lyft, Lumileds, Attology | 1\. Tech companies (Apple, Tesla, Oracle, etc); 2.
Banks (Morgan Stanley, Merrill Lynch, Wells Fargo, etc); 3. Consulting and VC
(Deloitte, Kleiner Perkins, Stout Risius Ross, etc)  | Apple, Amazon, Walmart,
Google, Facebook, Cisco, Oracle, Salesforce, Adobe, PayPal, eBay, VMware,
Intel, Visa, Accenture, KPMG, SAP, Intel  
**The SCU Difference** |  Practicum programs draws 20+ partners to sponsor
data science projects for students | STEM designated; flexible program
duration (10, 16, 24 months); vast alumni network  | Network of 400+ MSIS
Alumni in the Silicon Valley and around the world

[Open online application](https://slate.scu.edu/apply/)  | [Open online
application](https://slate.scu.edu/apply/)
