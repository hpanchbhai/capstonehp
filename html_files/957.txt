Shreyas Ramaswami

#### Finance Major

Shreyas Ramaswami is a Junior Finance major. He is the treasurer of Intandesh
and an active member of their bhangra team. In his free time, he loves playing
the Indian classical percussion instrument, spending time with friends, and
playing table tennis. Shreyas is extremely excited to be a part of the Peer
Adviser team and is looking forward to helping out all the incoming freshman
this year!
