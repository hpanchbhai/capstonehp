# 7th Annual West Coast Workshop in International Finance

![](/media/leavey-school-of-business/economics/wcwif/Untitled-1-359x359.jpg)

#### November 9, 2018  
University of California, Santa Cruz

  
The 7th annual West Coast Workshop in International Finance will be held at
The University of California, Santa Cruz on Friday, November, 9th.



* * *

  |

## **Agenda               **  
  
---|---  
8:30 - 9:00  |

 Coffee and Rolls  
  
  **International Debt** , _Chair_ : **Kenneth Kletzer** (UCSC)  
  
9:00 - 9:45 |

“[Signaling in Sovereign Debt
Markets](https://www.scu.edu/business/economics/news--
events/wcwifinternational-finance-workshop/stangebye--sovereign-debt-
markets/),” **Zachary Stangebye** (U. Notre Dame) and Mark Wright (FRB
Minneapolis)

  _Discussant: **Galina Hale**_ (FRBASF)  
  
9:45 - 10:30

|

“[International Reserves, Credit Constraints, and Systemic Sudden
Stops](https://www.scu.edu/business/economics/news--events/wcwifinternational-
finance-workshop/shousha---international-reserves/),” **Samer Shousha**
(Federal Reserve Board)

_Discussant:_ **Joshua Aizenman** (USC)  
  
10:30 - 10:45

| Break  
  
  **Monetary Policy,** _Chair_ : **Andrew Rose** (U.C. Berkeley)  
  
10:45-11:30 |

“[Golden Fetters and the Causal Effects of Countercyclical Monetary
Policy](https://www.scu.edu/business/economics/news--
events/wcwifinternational-finance-workshop/pina---countercyclical-monetary-
policy/),” Kris James Mitchener (SCU) and **Gonçalo** **Pina** (SCU)  

_Discussant:_ **Sanjay** **Singh** (UCD)  
  
11:30 - 12:15 |

 “[Commodity Currencies and Monetary
Policy](https://www.scu.edu/business/economics/news--
events/wcwifinternational-finance-workshop/smith---commodity-currency/),”
Michael B. Devereaux (UBC) and **Gregor W. Smith** (Queens University)

_Discussant:_ **Yu-Chin Chen** (UW)  
  
12:15 - 1:45  |

 Lunch  
  
  **Business and Financial Cycles** , _Chair_ : **Katheryn Russ** (U.C. Davis)  
  
1:45-2:30 |

 “[How ETFs Amplify the Global Financial Cycles in Emerging
Markets](https://www.scu.edu/business/economics/news--
events/wcwifinternational-finance-workshop/williams--efts-and-emerging-
markets/),” Nathan Converse (FRB), Eduardo Levy-Yeyati (U. Torcuato di Tella),
and **Tomas Williams** (GW)

  _Discussant:_ **Dongwon Lee** (UCR)  
  
2:30 - 3:15  |

 “International Linkages and the Changing Nature of International Business
Cycles,” Wataru Miyamoto (Bank of Canada) and **Thuy Lan Nguyen** (SCU)

_Discussant_ : **Chenyue Hu** (UCSC)  
  
3:15 - 3:30  |

 Break  
  
  **Currency, Trade, and Monetary Policy,** _Chair_ : **Helen Popper** (SCU)  
  
3:30-4:15 |

 “[Credit Migration and Covered Interest Rate
Parity](https://www.scu.edu/business/economics/news--
events/wcwifinternational-finance-workshop/liao--credit-migration/),” **Gordon
Y. Liao** (Federal Reserve Board)

_Discussant:_ **Grace Gu** (UCSC)  
  
4:15 - 5:00  |

 “[Optimal Capital Account Liberalization in China?](
https://www.scu.edu/business/economics/news--events/wcwifinternational-
finance-workshop/liu--capital-accounts/)” **Zheng Liu** (FRBSF), Mark M.
Spiegel (FRBSF) and Jingyi Zhang (Shanghai University)

_Discussant_ : **Manisha Goel** (Pomona College)  
  
5:00 - 7:30  |

Reception and  Dinner  
  
The workshop organizing committee includes: Yu-chin Chen, University of
Washington; Grace Weishi Gu, UC Santa Cruz; Chenyue Hu, UC Santa Cruz; Mark
Spiegel, Federal Reserve Bank of San Francisco; Romain Rancière, USC; Andrew
Rose, UC Berkeley; Katheryn Russ, UC Davis; Kenneth Kletzer, UC Santa Cruz
(Co-Chair); Helen Popper, Santa Clara University (Co-Chair). The workshop is
sponsored by the Department of Economics at Santa Clara University, the
Department of Economics at UC Santa Cruz, and the Federal Reserve Bank of San
Francisco.
