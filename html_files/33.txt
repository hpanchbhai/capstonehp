# Academic Departments

  1. [Home](/)
  2. [Leavey School of Business](/business/)
  3. [Academics](/business/academics/)
  4. Academic Departments 

The Leavey School of Business consists of six academic departments that house
8 majors and 5 minors in addition to 7 graduate degrees.



#####  [Accounting](https://www.scu.edu/business/accounting/)

Pursue a major in Accounting or Accounting and Information Systems. Our
accounting students are highly sought by national and regional accounting
firms.  
  

  * [Requirements](/business/accounting/academics/ "Home » Home » Leavey School of Business » Accounting » Academic Programs")
  * [Courses from Catalog](/business/accounting/academics/courses/ "Home » Home » Leavey School of Business » Accounting » Academic Programs » Undergraduate Courses")
  * [Faculty and Staff](/business/accounting/faculty/ "Home » Home » Leavey School of Business » Accounting » Faculty & Staff")

#####  [Finance](https://www.scu.edu/business/finance/)

The finance major at Santa Clara University opens all kinds of doors and will
prepare you for success in making and managing financial decisions.  
  

  * [Requirements](/business/finance/academics/ "Home » Home » Leavey School of Business » Finance » Academic Programs")
  * [Courses from Catalog](/business/finance/academics/courses/ "Home » Home » Leavey School of Business » Finance » Academic Programs » Undergraduate Courses")
  * [Faculty and Staff](/business/finance/faculty/ "Home » Home » Leavey School of Business » Finance » Faculty and Staff")

#####  [Management &
Entrepreneurship](https://www.scu.edu/business/management/)

Gain a broad perspective of the challenges faced by business today, and
develop the skills needed to become a leader in successful organizations,
large or small.  
  

  * [Requirements](/business/management/academics/ "Home » Home » Leavey School of Business » Management » Academic Programs")
  * [Courses from Catalog](/business/management/academics/courses/ "Home » Home » Leavey School of Business » Management » Academic Programs » Undergraduate Courses")
  * [Faculty and Staff](/business/management/faculty/ "Home » Home » Leavey School of Business » Management » Faculty and Staff")

#####  [Economics](https://www.scu.edu/business/economics/)

Choose from two degrees in economics: A Bachelor of Science in Commerce
through the Business School or a Bachelor of Science through the College of
Arts and Sciences.

  * [Requirements](/business/economics/academics/ "Home » Home » Leavey School of Business » Economics » Academic Programs")
  * [Courses from Catalog](/business/economics/academics/courses/ "Home » Home » Leavey School of Business » Economics » Academic Programs » Undergraduate Courses")
  * [Faculty and Staff](/business/economics/faculty/ "Home » Home » Leavey School of Business » Economics » Faculty and Staff")

#####  [Information Systems and Analytics](https://www.scu.edu/business/isa/)

Pursue a major in Management Information Systems (MIS), or Accounting and
Information Systems (AIS). You can also supplement any major with an MIS or
Business Analytics minor.

  * [Requirements](/business/isa/academics/ "Home » Home » Leavey School of Business » Operations Management & Information Systems » Academic Programs")
  * [Courses from Catalog](/business/isa/academics/courses/ "Home » Home » Leavey School of Business » Operations Management & Information Systems » Academic Programs » Undergraduate Courses")
  * [Faculty and Staff](/business/isa/faculty/ "Home » Home » Leavey School of Business » Operations Management & Information Systems » Faculty and Staff")

#####  [Marketing](https://www.scu.edu/business/marketing/)

Our marketing students find jobs in a wide range of areas from retail
advertising and sales to new product development and market research to
internet marketing and supply chain.

  * [Requirements](/business/marketing/academics/ "Home » Home » Leavey School of Business » Marketing » Academic Programs")
  * [Courses from Catalog](/business/marketing/academics/courses/ "Home » Home » Leavey School of Business » Marketing » Academic Programs » Undergraduate Courses")
  * [Faculty and Staff](/business/marketing/faculty/ "Home » Home » Leavey School of Business » Marketing » Faculty and Staff")
