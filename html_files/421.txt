About The Retail Management Institute

The Retail Management Institute (RMI) is a recognized retail industry partner
with a 36-year history and strong reputation for developing strategic leaders

![](/media/leavey-school-of-business/illustrations-mp/RMI-Logo-Update--
360x286.png)

with the advanced technology skills necessary for success in a digital retail
environment. Santa Clara University faculty members working with an Advisory
Board of industry innovators and luminaries represent RMIs vision of becoming
a globally visible thought leader and destination for digital retail
innovation.



##### Retail Studies Program

__

The Retail Studies Program minor provides undergraduates in Business, Arts &
Sciences, and Engineering with the necessary skills, knowledge, and contacts
to launch a successful career in the retail industry with positions in Digital
Marketing, eCommerce, Buying, Merchandising, Finance, Store Leadership/In-
store Operations, Supply Chain & Procurement, and Product Development. RMI
teams with an ever-expanding alliance of leading retail innovators and global
brand leaders to provide students industry exposure and business
opportunities. Students have an opportunity to engage directly with RMI
partnering companies from Chloe+Isabel, Cost Plus World Market, Crate &
Barrel, E & J Gallo Wines, Levi Strauss & Co., Lolli & Pops, Gap, inc.,
Orchard Supply Hardware, Ross Stores, Inc., Sephora, Stitch Fix, Target, The
Grove, Trader Joe's, Williams-Sonoma, as well as influential Silicon Valley
and San Francisco Bay Area companies including Apple, Google, Facebook, IBM,
Pinterest, Microsoft and LinkedIn.

##### Faculty and Staff

__

With extensive experience in both the retail industry and higher education,
the Retail Management Institute's faculty and staff are valuable resources for
assisting retail studies students with internship opportunities and jobs post-
graduation. Providing cutting-edge curriculum, scholarships, and an extensive
support network, the Retail Studies Program has developed nearly 800 graduates
into industry leaders. [Learn more about each of our nationally recognized
faculty and staff.](/business/retail-management-institute/about/faculty--
staff/)

##### RMI Research

__

Service to the retail industry is solidified in key area through the
institutes Retail research program, the [Retail Workbench Research and
Education Center](/business/retail-management-institute/about/research/). The
Workbench aims to expand knowledge and solve problems that impact the industry
using advanced information technologies and data analytics.

#####  Advisory Board

__

RMI has assembled CEOs, Presidents, SVPs, CMOs and Directors from influential
Silicon Valley and San Francisco Bay Area companies. The Advisory Board meets
twice a year to provide insights and perspective to ensure RMI remains a top
destination for a retail business education. [Learn more about RMI advisory
board members here.](/business/retail-management-institute/about/advisory-
board/).
