### GLOBAL JESUIT BUSINESS ETHICS CONFERENCE

**Thursday, July 11, 2019 – Saturday, July 13, 2019**

**Santa Clara University, Santa Clara, California USA**

### Sponsored by:

### Leavey School of Business, Santa Clara University

### Markkula Center for Applied Ethics, Santa Clara University

### Colleagues in Jesuit Business Education (USA)

  * Regular Conference Fee $550.00 (ends May 15, 2019)
  * Late Registration conference fee $600.00 (ends June 1, 2019)

  * For further information email [ethicsconference@scu.edu](mailto:ethicsconference@scu.edu) 
  * Any questions about submitting academic papers can be addressed to [conferencepapers@scu.edu](mailto:conferencepapers@scu.edu)

##### Agenda

__

![Illustration of man and woman holding bags walking away from
clock](/media/leavey-school-of-business/illustrations-
mp/Schedules-v2-360x250.jpg)

  * Regular Conference Fee $550.00 (ends May 15, 2019)
  * Late Registration conference fee $600.00 (ends June 1, 2019)

**Wednesday, July 10**

Campus Housing available for early arrivals.

**Thursday, July 11 – Day for Faculty Visits to Silicon Valley Companies**

1:00 pm           Leave Santa Clara Campus for company visits

1:30 pm           Tour and Presentation – Company A

3:30 pm           Tour and Presentation – Company B

5:30 pm           Return to Santa Clara Campus

6:00 pm           Opening Mass (optional)

7:00 pm           Opening Dinner – Adobe Lodge, Santa Clara University

**Welcome:**

Dean Caryn Beck-Dudley, Leavey School of Business

Nicholas Santos, SJ, Marquette University, CJBE Chair

Kirk O. Hanson, former Executive Director, Markkula Center



**Friday, July 12 – “Corporate Day” for Academics and Silicon Valley Business
Leaders**

7:45 am            Meditation (optional)

8:00 am            Breakfast

8:45 am            Keynote Talk: Jesuit Official

9:30 am            Corporate Panel 1

10:45 am          Coffee Break

11:00 am          Corporate Panel 2

12:30 pm         Lunch

2:00 pm           Keynote Talk: Corporate Executive

2:45 pm           Corporate Panel 3

4:00 pm           Coffee Break

4:15 pm           Working sessions in Small Groups

5:30 pm           Reception – Dowd Art and Art History Building, Santa Clara
University



**Saturday, July 13 – “Academic Day”**

7:45 am            Meditation (optional)

8:00 am            Breakfast

8:45 am            Plenary Panel on Teaching Business Ethics

10:00 am          Coffee Break

10:15 am          Paper Presentations in Parallel Sessions I

11:30 am          Paper Presentations in Parallel Sessions II

12:45 pm         Lunch

2:00 pm           Paper Presentations in Parallel Sessions III

3:15 pm           Coffee Break

3:45 pm           Plenary Panel on Critical Issues in Disruptive Technology

5:00 pm           Adjourn

6:00 pm           Buses depart for dinner

6:30 pm           Festive Dinner at Testarossa Winery, Los Gatos, CA



**Sunday, July 14**

Informal breakfast provided

Conference participants may depart Sunday or Monday
