## SCU's Annual Meet the Firms Night

Each fall quarter, the SCU Accounting Association hosts Meet the Firms night
where students are able to talk with representatives from accounting firms
looking to hire students for either internships or full-time positions.

Meet the Firms Night is the SCU Accounting Association’s annual career fair
that is held each fall quarter. This year’s event, organized by senior
Accounting Association officer Evan Chen, took place in Locatelli Hall on
Wednesday, September 27, 2018. Approximately 130 students met with
representatives from 28 firms interested in hiring students as either interns
or full-time employees following graduation. Here is a list of the firms that
attended the event:
