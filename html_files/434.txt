## Hersh Shefrin : Huawei and 5G : Forbes

Professor of Finance Hersh Shefrin wrote an op-ed published in Forbes
explaining the risks associated with Huawei’s 5G plans for Germany and the
U.K.



![Mario L. Belotti Professor of Finance Hersh Shefrin Head
Shot](/media/public/people/Shefrin-IMG_9614-2-140x180.jpg)

**Hersh Shefrin, Mario L. Belotti Professor of Finance, wrote an op-ed
describing the benefits and risks surrounding Huawei’s current 5G focus.
Shefrin states that while 5G will help technology advancement, there are
strong considerations such as availability bias that need to be kept in mind.
On the behavioral side, this availability bias requires searching relevant
information otherwise ignored.**

Read the [full article
»](https://www.forbes.com/sites/hershshefrin/2019/02/21/huawei-and-facing-up-
to-5g-related-cyber-risks/#486e22962672)

Learn more about [Professor Hersh Shefrin
»](https://www.scu.edu/business/finance/faculty/shefrin/)
