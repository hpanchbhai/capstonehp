# LSB Advisory Boards

  1. [Home](/)
  2. [Leavey School of Business](/business/)
  3. [About LSB](/business/about/)
  4. [Leadership](/business/about/leadership/)
  5. LSB Advisory Boards 

A distinguished group of industry leaders work with the Dean, faculty members,
and senior staff to develop programs that enhance business education at the
graduate and undergraduate levels, as well as improve industry processes and
techniques.

Advisory Board members lecture in the classroom, collaborate with faculty on
research projects, mentor students, and provide guidance in curriculum
development.
