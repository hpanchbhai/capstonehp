John Hansen

#### Vice President and Founding Member,  
TM Financial Forensics

John L. Hansen is a Vice President and founding member of TM Financial
Forensics, LLC (“TMF”), a nation business and litigation consulting company
providing business, management and litigation consulting services to companies
in a variety of industries.  Over his 27 year consulting career, John has
consulted with companies on economic, accounting, valuation and damages issues
in a wide variety of industries, such as semiconductors, software, consumer
electronics, licensed merchandise, and medical devices, as examples.

John has analysed the profitability of hundreds of companies over the last two
decades in a variety of circumstances, including: mergers and acquisitions,
breach of contract, class action, employment, intellectual property
infringement, antitrust and fraud investigations.  He has particular expertise
in the valuation, licensing and determination of infringement damages for
intellectual property, including patents, trademarks, trade secrets and
copyrights.  John has testified as an expert on financial and economic damages
issues in Federal and State courts across the country.  He has also assisted
clients with investigations before the United States International Trade
Commission (“ITC”) and has testified as an expert in the ITC addressing the
impact of unfair competition on U.S. markets.

Prior to TMF, John was a Director at Navigant Consulting and a Vice President
at Tucker Alan Inc. where he was a leader of Tucker Alan’s national
intellectual property practice.  John obtained his Bachelor of Science in
Commerce, degree in Finance and minor in Economics from Santa Clara University
in 1989.  John holds several professional certifications and is a Certified
Public Accountant, Certified in Financial Forensics, a Certified Licensing
Professional, and a Chartered Global Management Accountant.
