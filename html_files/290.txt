## Sean O'Keefe

#### Course Coordinator  
Lecturer, Marketing

* * *

Sean O'Keefe is Course Coordinator and Lecturer of Business Communication at
the Leavey School of Business at Santa Clara University. His responsibilities
include curriculum design for the MBA and undergraduate programs focusing on
effective communication in business contexts. He teaches graduate and upper-
level undergraduate classes in “academics meets business” pragmatic
environments. His teachings cover the following aspects of business
communication: presentation skills, business writing skills, networking,
personal brand, salary negotiation, video conferencing skills, conflict
management, pitch skills, and more.

Mr. O’Keefe is President of SRO & Associates, a business consulting firm that
specializes in sales growth, sales leadership development, sales & marketing
strategy, recruiting and developing millennials, business communication,
strategic partnerships, and more.

Before transitioning into academia and consulting, Mr. O’Keefe was the Vice
President of Business Development for a regional tech company where he had P&L
responsibility. Over a period of seven years and with 100% organic growth, he
doubled its staff and quadrupled its profit. Prior, Mr. O’Keefe led the
Premium Seating Department with the Oakland Athletics Baseball Company where
he and his team set several sales records in the luxury suite, group ticket
and corporate sponsorship categories over a seven year period.

Mr. O'Keefe received his undergraduate degree from the University of
California, Santa Barbara in 2002. During college, Mr. O’Keefe interned for
the Oakland Athletics, worked for the San Francisco 49ers in the Player
Personnel department and spent time working for the San Francisco Giants. In
2008, he graduated from the Santa Clara University Leavey School of Business
with a Masters in Business Administration with emphasis in International
Business and interest in Social Entrepreneurship. During Mr. O’Keefe’s
graduate studies, he and his team won the prestigious Belotti Award, which
honors the best report on business strategy.

_January 2017_
