## Mohammad Amin Morid

#### Assistant Professor, Information Systems & Analytics

__[Curriculum Vitae (CV)](/media/leavey-school-of-business/isa/Amin-Morid_CV
---02-15-2019.pdf)

* * *

Dr. Morid is an assistant professor of the Department of Information Systems
and Analytics in the Leavey School of Business at Santa Clara University since
2018. He teaches Information Systems courses at the undergraduate and master
level.

Dr. Morid’s research focus is on the intersection of information systems and
biomedical informatics. He is particularly interested in predictive analytics
for healthcare big data using machine learning techniques. He has also worked
on other big data analytics venues such as E-Banking, Fraud and Outlier
Detection, Personalization and Recommender Systems. His current research focus
is on leveraging temporal patient data (extracted from electronic health
records, medical claims, etc.) for healthcare predictive analytics. His
research has been published and presented in refereed journals and conferences
such as Journal of Biomedical Informatics, Information Retrieval Journal,
Journal of Electronic Commerce, American Medical Informatics Association
(AMIA) and Conference on Health IT and Analytics (CHITA). He has worked with
hospitals, insurance companies and banks in various projects.

Dr. Morid earned his Ph.D. in Information Systems from University of Utah in
2018. He received his B.S. degree in Software Engineering and M.S. degree in
Electronic Commerce from Amirkabir University of Technology (Tehran
Polytechnic).
