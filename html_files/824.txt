## Our Keynote Speaker

#### Hessam Nadji

#### President & Chief Executive Officer

#### Marcus & Millichap

A leading commercial real estate firm specializing in investment sales,
financing, research and advisory services. Founded in 1971, Marcus & Millichap
has grown to more than 1,800 investment sales and financing professionals with
offices throughout the United States and Canada.  

In 2017, the firm completed nearly 9,000 transactions with a sales volume of
over $42 billion. Nadji joined Marcus & Millichap in 1996 as vice president of
research and advisory services and positioned the firm as a leading provider
of market trends, analyses and expertise. Over the years, his role expanded to
include marketing and strategy, enabling him to play a key role in
establishing and growing Marcus & Millichap’s national brand. In 2010, Mr.
Nadji assumed the leadership role for all of the firm’s national specialty
brokerage divisions, which grew rapidly under his supervision. Marcus &
Millichap’s specialty divisions function as client service teams of
specialists with in-depth expertise in 12 real estate segments and achieved
sales of $21.5 billion in 2015. Mr. Nadji also played a leading role in the
preparation and execution of the firm’s IPO in 2013 as Marcus & Millichap’s
chief strategy officer. He was named president and CEO in April 2016. Mr.
Nadji is frequently sourced on behalf of the firm by national business media
outlets, including The Wall Street Journal, Investor’s Business Daily, Real
Estate Forum, CNBC, Fox Business TV, Bloomberg TV, and numerous commercial
real estate publications.

Prior to joining Marcus & Millichap, Mr. Nadji was senior vice president at
Grubb & Ellis, where he began his career in 1986. He received a Bachelor of
Science degree in information management and computer science from City
University in Seattle. Mr. Nadji is a member of the National Multi Housing
Councilexecutive committee, the Urban Land Institute, the International
Council of Shopping  Centers and NAIOP.

## Our Panelist Moderator

#### Stanford Jones

#### Executive Managing Director Investments

#### Marcus & Millichap

As one of the initial advisors of Institutional Property Advisors, Stanford
Jones joined Marcus & Millichap in 1980, specializing in the sale of
multifamily properties. In 1988, he was promoted to vice president
investments. Mr. Jones was promoted to executive vice president investments,
the highest distinction awarded to agents of the firm, in 2008.



![](/media/leavey-school-of-business/marcom/real-
estate/John-M.-Sobrato-360x360.jpg)

## John Michael Sobrato

#### CEO & Trustee

#### Sobrato Organization

Mr. John M. Sobrato serves as the Chief Executive Officer and Director of The
Sobrato Organization, LLC and served as its Chief Financial Officer. Mr.
Sobrato served as Chief Executive Officer of Sobrato Organization, LLC until
July 2013. Mr. Sobrato primary responsible for the strategic direction of the
Sobrato Organization's portfolio of over 15 million square feet of commercial
and residential income properties as well as its investments in global
marketable securities. Mr. Sobrato serves as President of Sobrato Construction
Corporation. He serves as Chairman of Sobrato Development Companies. Mr.
Sobrato serves as Director of Santa Clara University. He serves as Director of
The Tech Museum of Innovation. He also served as Chairman or President of KTEH
Public Television, Catholic Charities of San Jose, the San Jose Repertory
Theater, Charities Housing Development Corporation and the Board of Fellows of
Santa Clara University. He served as a Director of Silicon Valley Community
Foundation. He is very involved in the local community. He serves as Chair of
the Board of Regents of Santa Clara University, Chairman of the Board of
Directors of the Silicon Valley Community Foundation and Chairman of the Board
of Trustees of Bellarmine College Preparatory. Mr. Sobrato graduated from
Santa Clara University in 1983 with a BSC.

## Stephen E. Schott Jr.

#### Vice President

#### SCS Development Company

Stephen E. Schott is the Vice President of SCS Development Company, which
holds the divisions of Award Homes and Citation Homes Central. The company
focuses on construction projects spanning the geographical areas of Santa
Clara, Alameda, San Mateo, Monterey and San Joaquin counties.

Stone-360x360.jpg)

## Larry Stone

#### County Assessor

#### **Santa Clara County**

San Jose Magazine named him one of Silicon Valley’s 100 most powerful leaders.
He was first elected Assessor in 1994 and overwhelmingly re-elected five times
by the residents of Santa Clara County, which stretches north to Palo Alto,
south to Gilroy, and includes San Jose, the "Capital of Silicon Valley."  The
county boasts a population of 1.9 million - greater than that of 13 states.

Stone has been a financial manager on Wall Street and was co-founded a
successful Bay Area real estate investment and development firm. He served 16
years as a council member and mayor of Sunnyvale, a period during which the
city earned an international reputation for effectiveness and became a model
for President Clinton and Vice President Gore’s efforts to reform the federal
bureaucracy.

  

![](/media/leavey-school-of-business/marcom/real-estate/Hao-Ko-360x360.jpg)

## Hao Ko

#### Principal and Managing Director

#### ****Gensler****

As Managing Director for the San Francisco office of Gensler, Hao leads with
design and is focused on building a dynamic practice through an open and
collaborative culture. An inquisitive architect by nature, he is passionate
about transforming experiences based on a deep understanding through research,
the craft of technical excellence and a rigor of intellectual thought, all
while striving to design beautiful places that impact people for the better.
Working with diverse and talented teams, Hao’s projects such as The Tower at
PNC Plaza, NVIDIA Headquarters and Mercedes-Benz USA Headquarters have
elevated the firm’s national and international profile and are well known for
their high level of innovation and quality. He is an alumnus of UC Berkeley,
where he graduated with Highest Honors, and the Harvard University Graduate
School of Design.

![](/media/leavey-school-of-business/marcom/real-estate/Gary-
Filizet-360x360.jpg)

## Gary Filizetti

#### President

#### Construction at Devcon Construction

Filizetti oversees Devcon’s Construction and Architectural Design Departments,
and actively supervises preconstruction, estimating and construction. His
decades of construction experience and skill at building strong relationships
help him make valuable contributions to each of the 100+ projects active at
any given time.

Initially serving as Vice President of Estimating and Construction when he
joined Devcon as a partner in 1980, Filizetti led the transition of Devcon
from a developer that did some contracting into a large design/build general
contractor that does some developing. He became President in 1986, and has
been directly involved in producing tens of millions of square feet of tenant
improvements and building shells.



![](/media/leavey-school-of-business/marcom/real-estate/Lisa-
Gillmor-360x360.jpg)

## Lisa Gillmor

#### Mayor

#### Santa Clara

Lisa is a 4th generation Santa Claran, who currently serves as Mayor in the
City of Santa Clara. She has served 2 previous terms as Vice Mayor and City
Council Member from 1992-2000 and 2012-2016. She is a Real Estate Broker at
Gillmor and Associates/Public Property Advisors, a family owned real estate
management and consulting company in Santa Clara. Her business focus is in
Commercial Real Estate and Land and Surplus School District property, since
1982.

Lisa has been involved in many areas of government, non-profit and public
benefit organizations. She was a member of the Santa Clara County Grand Jury,
Santa Clara County Roads Commission, Santa Clara County Assessment Appeals
Board, City of Santa Clara Parks and Recreation Commission, Santa Clara
University Board of Fellows, and Mission City Community Fund Board.

In addition to her duties as Mayor, Lisa serves as the Chair of the Santa
Clara Stadium Authority and the Santa Clara Housing Authority.

![](/media/leavey-school-of-business/marcom/real-estate/Rob-
Wagner-360x360.jpg)

## Rob Wagner

#### Managing Director

#### Sares Regis Group of Northern California

Mr. Rob Wagner serves as Managing Director at SARES-REGIS Group. Mr. Wagner
serves as President of SARES-REGIS Multifamily Fund, L.P. Mr. Wagner Co-
founded Sares Regis Group of Northern California, LLC in 1992 and serves as
its Managing Director. He is responsible for the executive management of all
operating divisions in Northern California. He is co-founder of Regis Homes
Bay Area, LLC and serves as its Managing Director. He serves as President of
Sares Regis Western States Multifamily Fund. He serves as an Executive of
Sares-Regis Group, Investment Arm. He served as President and Chief Executive
Officer of Prometheus Development Co., Inc. He served as Senior Loan Officer
of Citicorp Real Estate, Inc. He served as a Director of The Swig Company. He
served as a Member of Urban Land Institute. He worked at Bay Area Council,
Fisher Center for Real Estate - Policy Advisory Board and Lambda Alpha
International. Mr. Wagner holds Bachelors Degree from Dartmouth College and
MBA from Boston University.
