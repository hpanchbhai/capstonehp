##### Requirements

__

There are no formal requirements for the program, but we recommend 1, 2 and 3
years of equity compensation experience for CEPI Levels I, II and III
respectively. For more information, please see the [program
description](/business/cepi/become-a-cep/exams/ "Home » Home » Leavey School
of Business » Certified Equity Professional Institute » Become a CEP »
Exams").

##### Program Fees

__

In order to register for an examination, the CEPI must receive your completed
registration with payment by the enrollment deadline. Registration cannot be
processed without payment. For additional questions about payment review the
CEPI [Handbook](/business/cepi/become-a-cep/study-materials/handbook/ "Home »
Home » Leavey School of Business » Certified Equity Professional Institute »
Become a CEP » Study Materials » CEPI Handbook") or contact
[cepi@scu.edu](mailto:cepi@scu.edu).

##### Exam Locations

__

**G** **eneral location information:**

Upon registration, you will be asked to pick an exam location. All exam
locations are subject to change and are routinely reviewed to best accommodate
the number of exam candidates. The actual location of an exam may be outside
of the city limits of the named city. You will be notified of the exact exam
location approximately three weeks prior to the exam.

#### US Exam Locations

Arlington, VA | Eden Prairie, MN | Orlando, FL  
---|---|---  
Atlanta, GA | Fairfield, CT | Philadelphia, PA  
Boston, MA | Greensboro, NC | Phoenix, AZ  
Chicago, IL | Houston, TX | Salt Lake City, UT  
Cincinnati, OH | Los Angeles, CA | San Diego, CA  
Cleveland, OH | Nashville, TN | Santa Clara, CA  
Dallas, TX | New York, NY | Seattle, WA  
Denver, CO | Northern NJ |  
Detroit, MI | Oakland, CA |  
  
#### **Non -US Exam Locations**

If you are registering outside of the US, please select "Non-US" as your
location. A representative from the CEPI will then be in contact with you.
Non-US exam locationa are arranged on an individual basis.  Candidates who
test outside of the US will be required to:

  * assist in arranging a suitable location
  * assist in securing a suitable proctor
  * allow other candidates in the vicinity to test at that location

Note that non-US exams may be administered on the Monday following the
regularly scheduled date. Candidates will be notified of the exam date at
least three weeks prior to the test.

##### Study Materials

__

Study Materials for the exams include a binder and several reference
textbooks. Your study binder is included in the cost of the exam and will be
sent via UPS to the address supplied during registration. The package will
require a signature for delivery. If you would like to change the address the
binder is sent to, please contact [cepi@scu.edu](mailto:cepi@scu.edu).

Reference textbooks need to be purchased separately. Confirmation of your
registration will include textbook ordering instructions. For more information
about ordering textbooks, please visit our [Study
Materials](/business/cepi/become-a-cep/study-materials/ "Home » Home » Leavey
School of Business » Certified Equity Professional Institute » Become a CEP »
Study Materials") page.

##### MyCEP

__

Your [MyCEP](https://mycep.scu.edu/cep/candidate/ "Home » Home » Leavey School
of Business » Certified Equity Professional Institute » Current CEPs  »
MYCEP") account is used to register for exams, check exam scores, update
personal information and defer exams.

Since the Certified Equity Professional Institute sends out communication
primarily through email, ensure that your primary email address (login email
address) remains up to date. If you have lost access to the primary email on
your MyCEP account, email [cepi@scu.edu](mailto:cepi@scu.edu) for assistance.

If you are having technical issues with the MyCEP system, email
[support@cepi.zendesk.com](mailto:support@cepi.zendesk.com).
