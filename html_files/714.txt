Retail Management Institute Scholarships

#### Application Process

![](/media/leavey-school-of-business/illustrations-mp/Business-
Buildings-216x177.png)





The application process starts in the beginning of the spring quarter. RMI
Scholarship Applications are available in the Retail Management Institute
Office, Suite 111 in Lucas Hall. For more information, call the RMI Office at
(408) 554-4753.







  * ![Dale Achabal](/media/leavey-school-of-business/rmi/Achabal-230x230.jpg)

#### Achabal Family Scholarship

This scholarship was formed in 1994 by the L.J. Skaggs Distinguished Professor
of Marketing and Executive Director of the Retail Management Institute,
Professor Dale Achabal, and his wife Carol Achabal.



  * ![Howard Lester](/media/leavey-school-of-business/rmi/Howard-Lester-230x230.jpg)

#### Howard and Mary Lester Scholarship

The Howard and Mary Lester scholarship was formed in 1998 through the Lester
Family Foundation and the late Howard Lester, former Chairman Emeritus of
Williams-Sonoma, Inc. It has supported over 30 students in the retail studies
program throughout the years.

  * ![Jill Curran](/media/leavey-school-of-business/rmi/Jill-Curran-230x230.jpg)

#### Jill Curran Retail Management Scholarship

The Jill Curran Retail Management Scholarship was formed in 2004 to honor and
remember beloved Santa Clara student Jill Curran, Class of 2004. Jill passed
away in the fall of 2002 while studying fashion retailing, marketing and
organizational behavior during a study abroad program in London. This
scholarship provides an opportunity for students in the Retail Management
program to attend the Annual National Retail Federation Convention and Expo
held annually in New York City.

  * ![Joseph Vesce](/media/leavey-school-of-business/rmi/Joe-Vesce-230x230.jpg)

#### Joseph Vesce Memorial Scholarship

The Joseph Vesce Memorial Scholarship was formed in 1995 in memory of a great
friend, mentor and former chairman of the Retail Management Institute advisory
board by his wife, Maria Vesce.

  * ![Ellen Magnin Newman](/media/leavey-school-of-business/rmi/Ellen-Magnin-Newman-230x230.jpg)

#### Ellen Magnin Newman Scholarship

As a founding member of the Retail Management Institute, this scholarship was
formed in 2001 in honor of Ellen Magnin Newman. Ellen's career in retail began
at Joseph Magnin Co., Inc as a buyer and merchandise manager.

  * ![Jack Kilmartin](/media/leavey-school-of-business/rmi/Jack-Kilmartin-230x230.jpg)

#### Jack Kilmartin Scholarship

As a founding member of the Retail Management Institute, this scholarship was
formed in 2001 in honor of Jack Kilmartin. Jack's retail career took him all
the way to chief executive officer of Mervyn's before passing in 2004.





  * ![John F. and Marguerite Kilmartin ](/media/leavey-school-of-business/rmi/letter-k-230x230.jpg)

#### John F. and Marguerite Kilmartin Scholarship

The John F. and Marguerite Scholarship was formed in 1983 from an endowment
set up by former founder and board member, Jack Kilmartin, in honor of his
parents. This scholarship has supported over 50 retail studies program
students since it's inception.

  * ![Philip-S.-Schlein.jpg](/media/leavey-school-of-business/rmi/Philip-S.-Schlein-230x230.jpg)

#### Philip S. Schlein Scholarship

As a founding member of the Retail Management Institute, this scholarship was
formed in 2001 in honor of Philip S. Schlein. Phil joined current company USVP
in 1985 after a successful 28-year career as an operating executive in the
retailing industry. Phil began his retail career with R.H. Macy, Inc which
lead him to be the President and CEO of Macy's California.

  * ![YMA](/media/leavey-school-of-business/rmi/YMA-230x230.jpg)

#### YMA Fashion Scholarship Fund

Organized by fashion industry leaders, FSF is a non-profit association that
provides scholarship grants to talented students interested to pursue a career
in any field in the fashion industry, such as design, merchandising,
marketing, business and finance. For more information visit the
[YMA](http://www.ymafsf.org/) online.
