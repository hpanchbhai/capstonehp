# Leavey School of Business

  1. [Home](/)
  2. Leavey School of Business 

## GO ANYWHERE FROM HERE

With undergraduate, graduate, and executive education covering all business
disciplines, the choice is yours.  
![home-page-img-artboard](/media/leavey-school-of-business/home-page-img-
artboard-01-545x334.png)





All programs at the Leavey School of Business provide rigorous study and high
impact experiential learning, culminating in rock-solid business acumen. Plus
the University’s Jesuit, Catholic tradition imbues all students with
unshakable ethics and a commitment to social responsibility. With our deep
integration with the myriad innovative industries in the Bay Area, this
combination makes us the perfect place to nurture the next generation of
business leadership.

Our faculty includes some of the most experienced industry professionals whose
goal is to help you understand and apply your field of study in context of the
vibrant Silicon Valley environment. And our alumni work at some of the most
recognized names in the world, so you have access to a vast network of like-
minded professionals who want to help you succeed. From Accounting to
Management and MIS, from full-time to part-time and evening programs, the
Leavey School of Business is the perfect business school to launch or
accelerate your career.

##### [Women's Corporate Board Readiness Program](/business/news/top-
stories/womens-corporate-board-readiness-program.html)

On September 30, 2018, Governor Jerry Brown signed SB 826 into law, mandating
that there be at least one woman on the board of directors in California-based
companies by the end of 2019. The Silicon Valley Executive Center will launch
the Women's Corporate Board Readiness Program May 2, 2019.

![](/media/leavey-school-of-
business/graduates/mba/fla_412004_09883-760x400.jpg)

##### [Higher rankings of LSB MBA programs in US News and World
Report](/business/news/top-stories/higher-rankings-of-lsb-mba-programs-in-us-
news-and-world-report.html)

Due to higher GMAT scores, greater work experience and better peer
assessments, LSB MBA programs significantly improve in US News and World
Report rankings.

![](/media/leavey-school-of-business/stories/VR4CIE-760x400.jpg)

##### [Innovation and Entrepreneurship Expands its Reach Across
Campus](/business/news/top-stories/innovation-and-entrepreneurship-expands-
its-reach-across-campus.html)

In a move to strengthen the “entrepreneurial mindset” necessary for
adaptability and reinvention, Santa Clara University’s Center for Innovation
and Entrepreneurship is expanding its reach and programming across campus.
