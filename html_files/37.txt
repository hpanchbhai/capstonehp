## YOU'RE LEARNING ON THE JOB, BUT THAT'S JUST NOT ENOUGH.

The Silicon Valley Executive Center at Santa Clara University gives you the
fast-paced practical learning that you need to advance your career and improve
your organization. Our world-renowned Silicon Valley faculty and practitioners
come together to help you gain the valuable skills, tools, and insights that
you must have to get to the next level.

#####  [Custom University  
Partnerships](https://www.scu.edu/business/edc/universitypartnerships//)

We partner with universities worldwide to provide access to the latest
business issues facing companies today.

#####  [Certificate of Advanced Accounting Proficiency
(CAAP)](https://www.scu.edu/business/caap/)

The CAAP program fosters an intimate learning environment with high-quality
faculty, and grants the necessary accounting units to sit for the California
CPA exam.

  * [Requirements](/business/caap/prospective/admissions/ "Home » Home » Leavey School of Business » Certificate of Advanced Accounting Proficiency » Prospective Students » Admissions Information")

In partnership with Dalton Education, the CFP Program offers courses that
fulfill the CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER™ certification education requirement
necessary to attain your CFP® certification.
