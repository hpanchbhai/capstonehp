## ABOUT THE SILICON VALLEY EXECUTIVE CENTER

![Illustration of people in building putting together a pie
chart](/media/leavey-school-of-business/edc/SM-Holistic-Pie_1-350x261.png)

The Silicon Valley Executive Center at Santa Clara University is more than
just executive education. It can be a game changer. We offer four robust
certificate programs to further your career. Or, if you want, change careers
entirely. We also offer partner companies custom corporate learning solutions
that align company teams with the latest knowledge of how business gets done
in Silicon Valley. So we’re much more than just a classroom. We’re a
laboratory where the future is being made.

![1.8k people annually, 7 countries worldwide, 25k hours of programming
annually](/media/leavey-school-of-business/edc/SVEC-Stats-01.png)

##### Silicon Valley Executive Center History

__

Since 1957, the Silicon Valley Executive Center at Santa Clara University has
leveraged its location and strong connections in the heart of Silicon Valley
to deliver customized learning solutions on topics ranging from innovation,
entrepreneurship and leadership, to high performing teams, managing a global
footprint, and the use of social networking in management.

##### Mission

__

The mission of The Silicon Valley Executive Center is:

  * Create positive visibility for the Leavey School of Business and Santa Clara University through programming activities.
  * Generate financial resources that can be utilized in supporting faculty research, as well as University and Leavey School of Business initiatives.

##### Goals

__

The Silicon Valley Executive Center provides lifelong learning opportunities
that:

  * Address the needs of the Silicon Valley business community
  * Enhance the competencies of managers to strengthen the global competitiveness of their firms
  * Further the mission of the Leavey School of Business (LSB) and the University
  * Create global visibility through partnerships with international set of universities
