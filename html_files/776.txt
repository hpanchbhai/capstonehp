Welcome to the CEPI Webinars for the 2018 exams. Use of this page is
restricted to current CEP exam candidates and CEP designees. Any other use is
prohibited.

#### GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS

  * Be sure to access the webinars on a device that is audio enabled.
  * You will be asked to register with ReadyTalk. This information is NOT shared with anyone. It is a means of capturing how many times the webinars are accessed. Note that you must register each time you access the webinar.
  * Once you have registered, the webinar will begin. It may take a minute to load depending on your internet connection speed.
  * To hide the player controls, click on the "Minimize" icon. To restore the controls, click anywhere on the screen.
  * For additional instructions, you can access the ReadyTalk "Help" menu.

Included with tuition is access to the CEPI Exam Overview Webinars. The CEP
Institute will open the webinars for use approximately five weeks before the
exam.

There is no limit to the number of times a candidate can access a session.
Candidates will be provided access information for all three webinars. Access
information will be distributed via email approximately five weeks before the
exam. To ensure the CEPI has your current email address, access your account
on MyCEP - [click here](http://www.scu.edu/business/cepi/mycep.cfm).

**Need more help studying?**

Don't forget to use the Review Guide in your binder.  This guide is a
comprehensive review of much of the reading for each level.

Remember to check out the Study Materials page for a list of [Educational
Resources](/business/cepi/become-a-cep/study-materials/#d.en.352965 "Home »
Home » Leavey School of Business » Certified Equity Professional Institute »
Become a CEP » Study Materials » ACC2-2: Educational Resources"). There you
will find links to exam prep classes and courses offered by third party
sources.

Be sure to read the [Certification Information
Handbook](/business/cepi/become-a-cep/study-materials/handbook/ "Home » Home »
Leavey School of Business » Certified Equity Professional Institute » The CEP
Program » Study Materials » CEPI Handbook"), which answers many non-exam
related questions. All candidates are responsible for adhering to the the
policies outlined in the handbook.

For information regarding course materials, please visit the [Study Materials
page](/business/cepi/become-a-cep/study-materials/ "Home » Home » Leavey
School of Business » Certified Equity Professional Institute » The CEP Program
» Study Materials").
