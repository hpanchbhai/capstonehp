## New Leavey School of Business Faculty Teaching in the Winter Quarter 2019

Lan Jiang and Mahum Hussain are new marketing professors in the winter
quarter. Also two new finance professors.

![](/media/leavey-school-of-business/undergraduates/ugbp-
newsletter/Mahum_SCU-175x175.jpg)

**Mahum Hussain** is a business and marketing professional with 12 years of
experience in training, mentorship, and conducting corporate workshops for
professionals and students in U.S., Singapore, and Pakistan.

Mahum is an academic lecturer by profession and has taught at Year Up Bay
Area, Kaplan Higher Education, ICRI Global Research, and Northern University.
She has been teaching Marketing, Management, Consumer Behavior, and
Communication-related subjects. In addition to her academic teaching, she also
volunteered in OPEN Silicon Valley, worked in Miller Center at Santa Clara
University, an advertising company in Pakistan, and a call center in
Singapore. She is holding BBA (hons.) and MBA degree from Hamdard University,
and specialized Diploma for Teachers and Trainers from Cambridge UK.

**"I am looking forward to creating a constructivist classroom and building an
environment where my students are actively engaged and learn about current
marketing trends and patterns."**

  

**Patrick Williams** , M.B.A., is a customer and consumer marketer who joins
Santa Clara University, following nearly 20 years working with leading brands
(e.g. Cheerios®, Betty Crocker®, Call of Duty® and Jacuzzi® Hot Tubs) and
strategic customers (e.g. Wal-Mart, Costco, Best Buy and Petco). Williams
holds a Master of Business Administration from The University of Southern
California and has taught undergraduates and post-graduate students at the
University of California-University Extension since 2013. New to the Bay Area,
Patrick, his wife, Lori, and family moved to the area in July of '17. So, when
he's not at the Leavey School of Business, Patrick is out getting acquainted
with the area.

**"Whether we realize or not, we all have an incredible amount of experience
in Marketing-- as Consumers. I'm excited to be in-class with undergraduates,
where we can apply concepts and the underlying strategy to their experiences.
Further, I'm excited to learn how they'll shape the future of marketing
through unique tools and technology."**



![](/media/leavey-school-of-business/undergraduates/ugbp-
newsletter/manish_tewariDONJ3496-175x175.jpg)

**Dr. Manish Tewari** is currently an adjunct lecturer of finance at Santa
Clara University's Leavey School of Business and an associate professor of
finance at Menlo College. Tewari’s major areas of research are in corporate
bonds, financial derivatives, and corporate social responsibility. He has
published papers on corporate bond provisions including publication in the top
journal in finance,   _Journal of Financial Economics_. His research in the
field of corporate social responsibility has led to publication in the
prestigious _  Journal of Business Ethics_.  Dr. Tewari has extensive teaching
experience in finance and has taught a wide variety of finance courses
including corporate finance, investments, money and banking, financial
modelling, and student investment funds. He has an undergraduate degree in
engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology, New Delhi and a Ph.D. in
finance from the University of Central Florida, Orlando, FL.



![](/media/leavey-school-of-business/undergraduates/ugbp-newsletter/Professor-
Ning-Pu-175x175.jpg)

**Dr. Ning Pu** received her Ph.D. in Finance from University of Massachusetts
Amherst in 2016. She taught Financial Management 121 in the Fall quarter of
2018 and will continue to teach Financial Management and another course -
Apply Financial Models to Financial Data - in the Winter quarter of 2019.
She worked as a research associate at NERA Economic Consulting's Finance and
Mass Tort Practice in NYC before getting her Ph.D. In her spare time, she
likes to play the piano and travel with her husband and their two kids.
