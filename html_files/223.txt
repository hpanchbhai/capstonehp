## Haidan Li

#### Department Chair  
Associate Professor, Accounting

* * *

Haidan Li is an associate professor and department chair of the accounting
department in the Leavey School of Business at Santa Clara University.

Her research and teaching interests are mainly in financial accounting,
corporate governance, and capital markets. She has published articles in
leading scholarly journals including _Journal of Accounting and Economics_ ,
_Contemporary Accounting Research_ , _Review of Accounting Studies_ , and J
_ournal of Law and Economics._ She received the American Accounting
Association Competitive Manuscript Award in 2003.

Dr. Li received her Ph.D. from the University of Texas at Austin, her M.A.
from Rutgers, The State University of New Jersey, and her B.A. from Zhongshan
University in China. She was an Assistant Professor at the University of Iowa
before joining Santa Clara University. She has received several teaching
awards at Santa Clara University, including the Professor of the Year Award
presented by the SCU Accounting Association in 2012.

_July 2015_
