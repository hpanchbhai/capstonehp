## 2017 Outstanding Graduates

The Accounting Department awarded the 2017 Outstanding Graduates at the 17th
Annual Accounting Awards Banquet on Thursday, May 25th in Locatelli Hall at
Santa Clara University.

![Outstanding Graduates 2017 Headliner](/media/leavey-school-of-
business/accounting/actg-news/AH4_8108-Pano-759x336.jpg)

The Accounting Department awarded the 2017 Outstanding Graduates at the 17th
Annual Accounting Awards Banquet on Thursday, May 25th in Locatelli Hall at
Santa Clara University.



![Arun Coumar](/media/leavey-school-of-business/accounting/actg-news/Round-
Arun.jpg)

This year’s recipient of the O. Robert Anderson Award is Arun Coumar. The
award was established in memory of accounting Professor O. Robert Anderson in
1973 and is presented to the accounting major judged outstanding in
scholarship by the faculty of the department. Arun Coumar is an accounting
major, Leavey Scholar, and Accounting Association officer from Seattle who
graduated in spring 2017. As a sophomore, Arun joined Presidio Bank as a
credit analyst who assessed complex client financials for credit covenant
maintenance. Presidio Bank was so impressed with Arun that they established a
new internship program with SCU that would continue the position that they
created for him. Arun has since joined PwC as an Associate in Capital Markets
and Accounting Advisory Services.

* * *



![](/media/leavey-school-of-business/accounting/actg-news/Round-
Thomas-285x368.jpg)



Awarded to Thomas Deal, the Jerry Kasner Memorial Award is given annually to
an outstanding accounting student at SCU selected by the Silicon Valley
Chapter of the California Society of CPAs. The award is named for Professor
Jerry Kasner, CPA and professor emeritus of law at Santa Clara University
School of Law. Tom is an accounting major from Lake Oswego, Oregon who minors
in economics and is a member of the university honors and Leavey Scholarships
programs. In his spare time, Tom enjoys watching or playing just about any
sport (though his favorites are basketball and football) and likes to travel
and experience new cities and cultures. Tom has since started as an Audit
Associate in KPMG's Portland office.

* * *



![Walter Yu](/media/leavey-school-of-business/accounting/actg-news/Round-
Walter.jpg)

The Accounting Department along with Accounting & Information Systems awarded
Walter Yu with the Outstanding Accounting & Information Systems Student Award.
Walter Yu is an A&IS major, ACE student, Accounting Association officer, and
member of the leadership honors program. His personal interests include
photography, cars, technology, and playing (and watching) tennis and
basketball. He also likes to go on hikes and traveling to new places. Walter
has left his mark on many people; it is difficult to find any professor or
accounting/A&IS student who has not interacted with him. Walter has since
joined PwC as an Associate in Capital Markets and Accounting Advisory
Services.
