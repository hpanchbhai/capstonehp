## JOIN THE CELEBRATION  
MARCH 26, 2019

Our Symposium is the ideal event for professionals who want to learn about the
most relevant industry topics, and network with their peers. We offer
carefully selected educational sessions in a unique campus environment. The
academic environment at the historic Santa Clara University campus breaks down
departmental and corporate barriers, and promotes interactions between
attendees, presenters, and sponsors. Attendees of all experience levels walk
away with useful information that can be immediately used in their job
functions.

To celebrate the 30th anniversary of the institute and 15th Annual CEP and
Silicon Valley NASPP Symposium, this year's Symposium will feature a look back
at the history--and a peek into the future--of the Certified Equity
Professional Institute. Register now [here](https://www.cvent.com/events/15th-
annual-cep-and-silicon-valley-naspp-symposium/registration-
fdadc53150c44ec9aca30fa4f6bf40e5.aspx?fqp=true)!

## Locations:

This year's Symposium will take place in the Leavey Center, home court for the
men's and women's basketball, and women's volleyball teams. All breakout
sessions will take place in Lucas Hall.

## Parking:

Your symposium registration includes on-campus parking. You do not have to pay
any additional parking fees. Please follow signs to the parking in front of
the Leavey Center. Should you have questions, there is an officer readily
available in the Main Entrance Kiosk. Please obey all parking restrictions
outside of the university lots, as parking is enforced by the city.
