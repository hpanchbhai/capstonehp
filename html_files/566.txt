Embracing the university’s teaching scholar model, the department encourages
its faculty to include topics addressed in our [Mission](/business/isa/about/)
in their research. In devoting significant energies to pushing the knowledge
frontier in information technology and business intelligence, the department
is a true steward of the spirit of Silicon Valley.
