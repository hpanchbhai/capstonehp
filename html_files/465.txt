Jeff Rademann

#### EVP & Regional President, Santa Clara Valley Region  
Wells Fargo & Company

Jeff Rademann is Wells Fargo’s President of the Silicon Valley Region and
26-year financial services veteran. Rademann has responsibilities for 70 Wells
Fargo branches in Santa Clara, San Mateo and Santa Cruz counties. Rademann
directs all aspects of retail consumer banking, small business and affluent
business.

Rademann is active in the community and member of the Board of Directors with
the Silicon Valley Education Foundation and Habitat for Humanity East
Bay/Silicon Valley.

Rademann is also an advisory board member at Santa Clara University’s Leavey
School of Business.

He’s a graduate of Boston University with a Master of Science in Banking and
Financial Services.
