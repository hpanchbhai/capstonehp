## Rohit Dube

#### Pricing and Product Strategy Consultant

* * *

Rohit is currently an internal pricing consultant at an S&P 500 technology
company.

Previously, Rohit spent several years in Strategy and Product Management roles
at Tintri, FireEye and Cisco. At Tintri, Rohit worked with the senior
executive team on pricing and profitability improvement initiatives. At
FireEye, Rohit’s contributions included setting pricing strategy and
developing a plan to transition the product portfolio from a basket of point
products to a platform. At Cisco, Rohit was responsible for software product
management of the firewall product portfolio - a $1B/year business. He also
led the turnaround of Cisco’s $250M/year Intrusion Prevention product line and
the go-to-market for Cisco’s $300M/year Data Center security business.

In an earlier life, Rohit was a Strategy Consultant at Bain & Co. where his
projects included IT Strategy for a Fortune-500 firm, private equity due-
diligence on a publicly held service provider and business case development
for a technology product company entering the managed services space.

Rohit holds an MBA from the Haas School of Business at the University of
California, Berkeley, where he graduated at the top of his class. He also has
a B.Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering from the Indian Institute of
Technology, Bombay and an M.S. in Computer Science from the University of
Maryland, College Park.
