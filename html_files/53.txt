## Higher rankings of LSB MBA programs in US News and World Report

Due to higher GMAT scores, greater work experience and better peer
assessments, LSB MBA programs significantly improve in US News and World
Report rankings.

**SANTA CLARA, Calif**., March. 12, 2019—Santa Clara University's Leavey
School of Business (LSB)  is proud to announce that its part-time Master of
Business Administration program has been ranked  **#22**  by  _US News & World
Report _in its _  2020 Best Graduate Schools_ rankings, a jump of 17 spots
over last year's ranking.

This ranking puts Santa Clara's MBA program among the top 8% of the 287
eligible part-time programs in the US and also:

  * **2nd**  in the San Francisco Bay Area
  * **2nd**  among all Jesuit Schools
  * **4th**  in the state of California

**Higher GMAT Scores,  Greater Work Experience and Better Peer Assessment have
significantly enhanced the reputation and success of the Leavey School of
Business. **

The Santa Clara LSB Executive MBA program has also received notable  _US News
and World Report_  rankings:

  * **1st  **among all Jesuit Schools
  * **13th**  in the  _2020 U.S. News & World Report Best Graduate Schools _rankings

"We are tremendously proud that our hard work leveraging the assets of our
school - our Silicon Valley network of leaders, our world-class faculty, and
students who bring experience to the classroom - is being recognized by our
peers and by national ranking," said Caryn Beck-Dudley, Dean of LSB.

LSB's Part Time MBA specialties have also been ranked high in the following
areas:

  * **16th**  in Finance
  * **17th**  in Marketing
  * **23rd**  in Information Systems
  * **24th**  in Entrepreneurship

"It's great to see deans from across the U.S recognizing how strong and
innovative our MBA programs are," said Toby McChesney, senior assistant dean
of Graduate Business Programs.

**About Leavey School of Business**  
Located in the heart of Silicon Valley, one of the most dynamic business
environments in the world, the Leavey School of Business combines academic
excellence in the 450-year Jesuit tradition, with an energetic, innovative
spirit that typifies the region. For more information, see our
[website](/business/ "Home » Home » Leavey School of Business").

**About  Santa Clara University**  
Santa Clara University, a comprehensive Jesuit, Catholic University located 40
miles south of San Francisco in California's Silicon Valley, offers its more
than 9,000 students rigorous undergraduate curricula in arts and sciences,
business, and engineering; master's degrees in business, education, counseling
psychology, pastoral ministry, and theology; and law degrees and engineering
doctoral degrees. Distinguished nationally by one of the highest graduation
rates among all U.S master's universities, California's oldest operating
higher-education institution demonstrats faith-inspired values of ethics and
social justice. For more information, see our [website](/business/ "Home »
Home » Leavey School of Business").
