## Masters in Financial Analytics (MSFA)

### Take control of the volatile finance industry.

Finance is at the core of successful innovation. Because without financial
leadership, ideas don’t get funded, products don’t get to market, and
profitability is elusive. Our targeted MS in Finance program has helped
countless professionals push their personal envelopes to excel in the
corporate world, master in investment management, and command capital markets.
We have part time programs to get you on a fast track to financial
proficiency.

Program Overview

• Program duration: 10 month full-time program; a part-time option is also
available.

• Time/schedule: Evening and Saturday classes.

• Course delivery: Cohort style with elective options.

![](/media/leavey-school-of-business/illustrations-mp/Control-room-
version-360x200.jpg)

### Challenge yourself to lead and make a difference in Silicon-Valley.

Here you’ll be surrounded by and learn from perhaps the world’s most dynamic
faculty members in a creative ecosystem. The program will equip you with the
foundational skills to make organizations better.
