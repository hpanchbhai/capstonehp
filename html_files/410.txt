CIE Advisory Board  An Advisory Board helps guide the development of the CIE’s
strategic direction and assists with fundraising to sustain the Center’s
operations. The Advisory Board provides the CIE with a 360 degree perspective
of the Silicon Valley ecosystem by bringing together leading Santa Clara
University faculty, entrepreneurs, venture capitalists, legal advisors, and
corporate executives.
