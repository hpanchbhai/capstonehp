# Advisory Board

  1. [Home](/)
  2. [Leavey School of Business](/business/)
  3. [Evening MBA](/business/evening-mba/)
  4. Advisory Board 

Founded in 2018, the MBA Alumni Advisory Board focuses on key strategic
initiatives that enhance our top ranked MBA Program. The goal of the board is
to support our focus on student engagement, corporate connections and
curriculum updates that are essential to drive the program to new heights. The
Board will work with the Dean, the MBA Faculty Director and Sr. Assistant Dean
of Graduate Business Programs as well as students and alumni of the MBA
program.
