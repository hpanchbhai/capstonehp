## Student, Accounting Seminar Series 2016-2017

The Department offers seminar series in the Fall and Winter Quarters for
students who are interested in developing a broader understanding of
accounting-related business issues.

The Department offers seminar series in the Fall and Winter Quarters for
students who are interested in developing a broader understanding of
accounting-related business issues.  These seminars introduce students to
current issues in accounting that may not be included in the regular
curriculum.  If you would like to suggest a topic or make a presentation,
please contact Professor Yongtae Kim.  Below is the list of speakers and their
presentation topics during the past academic year.
