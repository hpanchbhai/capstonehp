Nayana Movva

#### Chair  
Graduate Student Association

_Evening MBA '20 (expected)_

Nayana Movva is part of the Spring 2017 MBA cohort, concentrating in Finance.
She has 3 years of experience working as a Product Manager at Workday in Core
Financials. She looks forward to transitioning into the Venture Capital space
and is now working in Partnerships, Developer Relations at Twitch. In
addition, she also co-owns and manages two restaurants in Sunnyvale, CA.

Nayana previously served as President of SCU’s graduate Women in Business
organization (2017-2018) and was on the leadership team for Women @ Workday,
where she helped bring opportunities to women and minorities in the workplace
through meaningful events and initiatives. As GSA Chair, her goal is to
represent the student body in making the Graduate Business experience
fulfilling and enjoyable through providing professional and social events. She
also aims to provide a support system and network for graduate student led
organizations in the Leavey School of Business.

Nayana has a B.S. in Environmental Science from the University of California,
Riverside. She was born in India and raised in Cupertino, where she currently
lives. In her leisure time, she enjoys carpentry, oil-painting, yoga,
meditation, and traveling.

Connect with Nayana:  
Email: nmovva@scu.edu  
LinkedIn: https://www.linkedin.com/in/nayanataramovva/
