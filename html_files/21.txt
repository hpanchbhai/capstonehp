## Jump to the head of your class

Go Anywhere From Here. Any place, any industry from Supply Chain to Finance.

A master’s degree from the Leavey School of Business is your fast pass to a
more fulfilling career. Here you’ll learn from the best. Put innovative
business practices to work. And develop the skills needed to thrive in any
professional setting. Our alumni work at the top firms in the world—earning
some of the highest salaries in the nation. Opportunity’s knocking.

Do you hear it?
