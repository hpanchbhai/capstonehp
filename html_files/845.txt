## OMIS Ranked Top 20 in the Nation

Programs in the Operations Management and Information Systems department at
Santa Clara University’s Leavey School of Business have been ranked among the
top 20 in the country by U.S. News and World Report and College Factual.

Santa Clara University’s Management Information Systems (MIS) major was
recently recognized by _U.S. News and World Report_ and _College Factual_. The
major, housed in the Leavey School of Business’ Operations Management and
Information Systems (OMIS) department, was ranked No. 4 for [highest paid MIS
graduates](https://www.collegefactual.com/majors/business-management-
marketing-sales/mis-management-information-systems/rankings/highest-paid-
grads/) and No. 12 for [top MIS
colleges](https://www.collegefactual.com/majors/business-management-marketing-
sales/mis-management-information-systems/rankings/top-ranked/) in the United
States by _College Factual_ , and came in at No. 16 nationally (No. 1 in
California) for best undergraduate MIS program by [_U.S. News and World
Report_](https://premium.usnews.com/best-colleges/rankings/business-
management-information-systems).

“We aim to position students at the forefront of the industry and prepare them
for success in their careers,” said Haibing Lu, OMIS department co-chair. “Our
faculty, rich in industry experience and interdisciplinary expertise, do an
excellent job in this. Their research and teaching really help to elevate the
caliber of our graduates. We are very proud of the success of our program.”

This year, the Leavey School of Business was ranked No. 10 in _Money Magazine_
’s [Best Colleges for Business Majors](https://www.scu.edu/business/news/top-
stories/leavey-school-ranked-10-in-the-country-for-undergraduate-business-by-
money-magazine.html) rankings, came in at No. 2 in California and No. 16
nationally in the Best Undergraduate Business Schools by [_Poets &Quants for
Undergrads_](https://www.scu.edu/business/news/top-stories/leavey-school-of-
business-undergraduate-program-ranked-no-16-in-the-nation.html), and ranked
No. 64 nationally according to the [2018 _U.S. News and World Report_ Best
Colleges](https://www.scu.edu/business/news/top-stories/leavey-school-of-
business-ranked-among-top-100-business-programs-nationally.html) rankings.

Santa Clara University came in at [No. 2 Regional University in the
West](https://premium.usnews.com/best-colleges/rankings/regional-universities-
west), earning an overall score of 99 out of 100. The University was also
ranked No. 1 in [Best Colleges for Veterans](https://premium.usnews.com/best-
colleges/rankings/regional-universities-west/veterans), and No. 14 in [Best
Value Schools](https://premium.usnews.com/best-colleges/rankings/regional-
universities-west/best-value) by _U.S. News and World Report_.

Operations Management and Information Systems (OMIS) is an interdisciplinary
department that considers how technology can facilitate business decisions to
guide organizations to success. The program connects students with faculty who
have experience founding and managing companies, whose expertise spans both
engineering and management, and who are presently advancing the field through
their research.

Learn more about the OMIS department at SCU at
[https://www.scu.edu/business/omis/](/business/isa/ "Home » Home » Leavey
School of Business » Operations Management & Information Systems")
