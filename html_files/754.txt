![Illustration of an airplane flying into a city](/media/leavey-school-of-
business/edc/travel-01.png)

**Program Dates**

  * One week residential program in Germany: July 17-20, 2019
  * Monthly interactive webinars: 6 Webinars
  * Five day program at Santa Clara University: March 30-April 3, 2019  



**Locations**

In Germany:

Deggendorf Institute of Technology, Edlmairstrasse 6+8 Deggendorf 94469
Germany

In Silicon Valley:

Santa Clara University, 500 El Camino Real, Santa Clara, California- CA 95053-
0400, USA



##### Who Should Attend?

__

  * Entrepreneurs and entrepreneurial venture teams who want to lower the risk of their venture, raise capital and scale
  * Intact corporate teams focused on commercializing innovations (intrapreneurs)
  * Academic institutions who want to offer a program on commercializing disruptive innovations

##### Program Outline

__

**Themes that will be covered:**

  * Business Model Canvas
  * Market validation & Go-to-market strategies
  * Customer development
  * Lean Start-up methodology
  * Rapid prototyping
  * Financial modeling
  * Competitive analysis
  * Raising capital
  * Scaling your company
  * Elevator pitches
  * Investor pitches
  * The Silicon Valley mindset
