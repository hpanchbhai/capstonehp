# Undergraduates

  1. [Home](/)
  2. [Leavey School of Business](/business/)
  3. Undergraduates 

Academic achiever. Community leader. Entrepreneurial thinker. Environmental
steward. Globally aware. Professionally polished. These are the hallmarks of a
Leavey School of Business graduate.

Through your liberal arts core curriculum and your business major courses,
you'll acquire [expertise in a field of
study](/business/undergraduates/academics/majors-and-minors/ "Home » Home »
Leavey School of Business » Undergraduates » Academics » Majors and Minors"),
learn how your work will affect [your
community](/business/undergraduates/community/ "Home » Home » Leavey School of
Business » Undergraduates » Community & Campus Involvement") and the world at
large, and become a confident professional we'll be proud to call a Leavey
School of Business alumnus.

Logo](/media/leavey-school-of-business/stories/Image-for-Press-
releaseV2-4-360x189.png)
