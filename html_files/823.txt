Leavey School of Business Real Estate Symposium Agenda Wednesday, May 9th,
2018 || 4:00pm to 6:30pm || Louis B. Mayer Theatre

6:30pm to 9:00pm || Sculpture Garden, Edward M. Dowd Art & Art History
Building

     

##### Program

__

###  

### 4:00 PM  Doors Open

### 4:30 PM   Program Begins

### 4:30 - 4:45 PM  Introduction & Welcome

Lisa Kloppenberg, Dean of the School of Law

Steve Schott Jr., Vice President of SCS Development Company

### 4:45 - 5:00 PM  Market Update

Larry Stone, County Assessor of Santa Clara County

### 5:00 - 5:30 PM  Keynote Speaker

Hessam Nadji, CEO of Marcus & Millichap

### 5:30 - 5:35 PM  Introduction to Real Estate Association

Matt Jacobs & Peter Nestor, Co-Presidents of the Real Estate Association

### 5:35 - 5:55 PM  The Game Changing Impact of the Driverless Car on Real
Estate and the Future of Cities

Hao Ko, Principal and Managing Director of Gensler

### 5:55 - 6:30 PM  Panel: Designing, Developing, and Delivering Real Estate
Projects in High Growth Technology Markets

**_Panelists -_**

  * _Moderating our panel is Stanford Jones, Exec Managing Director  Investments for Marcus & Millichap and Institutional Property Advisors (IPA)_
  * _John Michael Sobrato, CEO & Trustee at Sobrato Organization_
  * _Gary Filizetti, President of Construction at Devcon Construction_
  * _Lisa Gillmor, Mayor of Santa Clara_
  * _Rob Wagner, Managing Director at Sares Regis Group of Northern California_
  * _Hao Ko, Principal and Managing Director of Gensler_

### 6:30 PM  Networking Event

Sculpture Garden, Edward M. Dowd Art & Art History Building

##### Parking

__

Parking will be directed to the nearby, 'North Campus Garage' at Alviso and
Benton. (GPS Info: 1063 Alviso St. Santa Clara 95050).

Overflow parking will be available in the university’s Main Parking Structure
at the entrance at 500 El Camino Real, Santa Clara, CA 95053. Should you have
questions, there is an officer readily available in the Main Entrance Kiosk.
