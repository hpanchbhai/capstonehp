# Evening MBA

  1. [Home](/)
  2. [Leavey School of Business](/business/)
  3. Evening MBA 

![](/media/leavey-school-of-business/graduates/mba/US-News-760x198.jpg)

Santa Clara University's Leavey School of Business (LSB) is proud to announce
that its part-time Master of Business Administration program has been ranked
**#22**  by  _US News & World Report _in its _  2020 Best Graduate Schools_
rankings, a jump of 17 spots over the last year's ranking.

![US News and World Reports PTMBA 2020](/media/leavey-school-of-
business/graduates/mba/US-News-PTMBA-150x160.png)

This ranking also puts Santa Clara's part-time MBA program:

  * Among the top **8%** of the 287 eligible part-time programs in the US
  * **2nd**  in the San Francisco Bay Area
  * **2nd**  among all Jesuit Schools
  * **4th**  in the state of California

LSB's Part Time MBA specialties have also been ranked high in the following
areas:

  * **16th**  in Finance
  * **17th**  in Marketing
  * **23rd**  in Information Systems
  * **24th**  in Entrepreneurship

## There's no shortcut to success

### Our graduate programs offer the boost you need.

Evening MBA. When we say Northern California innovation walks through our
doors after hours, we mean it. Working professionals are attending our evening
MBA program and earning degrees in two years. It’s a part-time program that
harnesses the influence of global business models to deliver an exceptional
education based on real-world applications. Our MBA gives you unprecedented
access to a rich network of peers, professionals, and professors in leadership
roles. They are today’s entrepreneurial vanguard who work in some of the
world’s most innovative companies.

Program Overview

• Program duration: Two years, from full-time to part-time.

• Time/schedule: Evening classes meet twice per week.

• Course delivery: Core courses are cohort style; elective courses are
flexible.

![](/media/leavey-school-of-business/illustrations-
mp/Mountain-360x200-360x199.png)

### Silicon Valley walks through our door at 5:00pm.

Here you’ll be surrounded by and learn from perhaps the world’s most dynamic
entrepreneurial ecosystem. You’ll be exposed to topics ranging from apparel to
analytics. Economics to entertainment. And fabrication to finance.
