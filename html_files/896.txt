Sadhvi Isanka

#### Co-President

_MBA Student_

Sadhvi is currently pursuing an MBA at Santa Clara University. She studied
Electronics and Communication Engineering from Anna University, Chennai. She
worked as Assistant Software Engineer in Tata Consultancy Services for two
years but departed to pursue an interest in fashion designing. After receiving
her degree in fashion designing in 2013, she started a boutique for Indian
couture creating traditional clothing collections. Sadhvi moved to the United
States in 2017 and discovered a desire to hone her entrepreneurial techniques
and learn new skills in the field of finance. She is looking forward to
applying all newly learned skills in the corporate finance field. Sadhvi’s
interest also include photography and travel.
