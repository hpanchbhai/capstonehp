## We put you in charge of your CAREER

![](/media/leavey-school-of-business/illustrations-mp/Career-Management.jpg)

Whatever your future professional aspirations may be, Graduate Business Career
Management will partner with you to successfully launch and manage the next
phase of your career.

**Beyond B-School**  
The on demand virtual platform of Beyond B. School is available at your
fingertips 24/7. Watch the videos when it works for your schedule and view
relevant videos on all aspects of the career and job search starting with
self-assessment to creating your personal brand with resumes, cover letters
and social media, to choosing companies and interviewing, to negotiating
offers. To get started, create an account at:  
<http://santaclara.beyondb-school.com/Login.aspx>

#### Leavey Industry Connects

**Netflix for Your Career!  
** GBCM is excited to provide free access to Firsthand Career Webinars. The
library features 100's of recorded webinars from top experts like Gayle
McDowell (author of the Google Resume) and Steve Dalton (author of The 2-Hour
Job Search). Log in with your student email address here: <https://scu-
lsb.firsthand.co>

**Vault Online Career Library**

Vault is an educational tool that provides you unlimited, online access to
comprehensive career information and management tools. It includes access to
industry and functional job information, Vault job board, company profiles,
and career tips.
