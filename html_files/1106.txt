Julie Cavanna-Jerbic

#### Managing Director, Iron Butterfly Consulting

Julie Cavanna-Jerbic is a senior operations executive, qualified financial
expert, and corporate strategist recognized for leading complex organizations
by aligning strategy and growth objectives with corporate goals.  A former
CFO, GM, and CIO at Hewlett-Packard Company (HP), Julie directed top-level,
high profile initiatives in Finance, Sales Operations, and IT that streamlined
operations across organizations.

As the Managing Director of Iron Butterfly Consulting, LLC, Julie Cavanna-
Jerbic currently advises leadership teams on effectively managing transactions
and market disruptions. Julie is known for her expertise in technology-based
businesses including Services, SaaS, Cloud, Data Analytics, and ERP
Initiatives. Her signature workshop, The Pragmatic & Strategic CFO, allows
professionals to develop skills to manage across the enterprise and function
at the COO level with a focus on increasing shareholder value. Julie shares
her expertise nationally by offering business advisory services to make your
business thrive in the face of change, executive education to build your
leaders of tomorrow, and executive coaching to create career resilience in
your team.

Julie is a past president of Financial Executives International Silicon Valley
(FEI SV) Chapter. Under her leadership, FEI Silicon Valley Chapter initiated
the FEISV CFO Readiness Academy in 2014 and received four National Awards in
2017. Julie is currently on the Supervisory & Audit Committee of First Tech
Federal Credit Union, 6th largest in the nation with $12B of assets under
management.  Julie is also a member of the Board of Trustees of Morrissey
Compton, a non-profit corporation assisting children with learning
differences. Most recently, she completed her service on the Board of LotLinx
as Audit Committee Chair and Compensation Committee member. She is a noted
speaker on Accounting, Finance, and IT Transformations, leading panels at UC
Berkeley’s Haas School of Business, Santa Clara University, and in other
forums.

Julie Cavanna-Jerbic earned a B.A. in Economics and French Literature from the
University of California at Berkeley graduating with High Distinction and Phi
Beta Kappa, an M.B.A. in Corporate Finance and Marketing from the University
of Southern California and completed the Directors’ College at the Rock Center
for Corporate Governance at Stanford University Law School.
