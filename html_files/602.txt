**Senior Leadership Academy Application 2018 - 2019**



Are you ready to embark on a 6-month long journey of personal development,
growth, and reflection with fellow seniors? To learn more about Senior
Leadership Academy's unique and immersive program offerings, [click
here](https://www.scu.edu/business/undergraduates/community/senior-leadership-
academy/), or take a look at past students' experiences described in the
videos below.



**Senior Leadership Academy Application Timeline:**

October 1: Application portal opens  
October 15: Applications due at noon  
October 19: Selected participants notified  
October 26: Deadline to confirm participation  
November 1: Academy orientation  
November 13: Mentor Matching

To be eligible for the Senior Leadership Academy, you must attend a mandatory
retreat from Friday, January 11 through Sunday, January 13, 2019 located off-
campus.

Should you have questions about the application or process, please contact
Brenda Versteeg, [bjversteeg@scu.edu](mailto:bjversteeg@scu.edu), or visit the
Undergraduate Programs Office, Lucas Hall 115.
