**Getting Started with Career Consulting**

Want help deciding or implementing your career path? Wondering how to pivot to
a new direction? Want to practice your interviewing skills?

To set up an appointment for a career consulting session or resume review, or
request a CareerLeader account, complete [the appropriate
form](/business/graduates/career-management/students/career-leader-request-
form/ "Home » Home » Leavey School of Business » Current Students » Career
Management » Students » Career Leader Request Form"). GBCM will follow up with
you within two business days on next steps for setting up your appointment.

GBP students may meet Career Consultants anytime throughout the year for
confidential 30-minute career consulting sessions in Lucas Hall 116. To
schedule an appointment, find an open career consultation session with one of
the following Career Consultants:

**Career Lounge**

Have a quick or urgent career question? Drop by the GBCM Career Lounge to
speak with a Career Consultant in the B of A Commons on select Wednesdays and
Thursdays between 4:30 - 5:45 PM. Topics you may want to discuss with your
coach include:

![Career Planning image cap](/media/leavey-school-of-business/illustrations-
mp/Career-Planning-360x360.jpg)

#### Career Planning

Learn to map out a 3-5 year plan. Use Career Leader self-assessment tools to
help identify your interests, motivators and skills and suggest recommended
career path. Set professional development goals.

![Career Enhancement image cap](/media/leavey-school-of-
business/illustrations-mp/Career-Transition-360x360.jpg)

#### Career Enhancement

Plan and implement an effective job search strategy; Identify transferable
skills; Identify networking opportunities; Create effective resumes and cover
letters; Use online resources to supplement your search.

![Career Acceleration image cap](/media/leavey-school-of-
business/illustrations-mp/Career-Acceleration-360x360.jpg)

#### Career Acceleration

Leverage your MBA/MS degree and conduct a job search to accelerate your
career. Identify your leadership style, strengths and areas for improvement.
Communicate effectively with your teammates.
