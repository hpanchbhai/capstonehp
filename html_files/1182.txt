### **West Coast Workshop in International Finance 2016**

#### **The 5th annual West Coast Workshop in International Finance will be
held at:  
Santa Clara University on Friday, October 14th.  
Forbes Room - Lucas Hall 106**

**[[Map](http://www.scu.edu/map/148/Lucas+Hall)]**

Discussant:  **Benjamin Hébert** (Stanford)  
  
  |

  |

**International Equity Markets** , Chair: Alex Mandilaras (U. Surrey)  
  
2:00-2:30 |

“[Four centuries of return predictability](/media/leavey-school-of-

“[Sectoral Productivity Matters: A New Angle on Equity Home
