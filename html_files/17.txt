This market demand leads us to develop programs that grow business leaders.
Fifty-three of the Fortune 500 have offices in the San Francisco Bay Area. And
we’re called on by businesses of all types to support their need for world-
class leadership. And for those seeking a master of science (MS) degree, we
created curricula designed for today’s most sought-after business specialties,
including business analytics, finance, information systems, and supply chain
management and analytics.  You choose; you excel.

### Graduate Business Degrees

#####  [New Graduate Application](https://slate.scu.edu/apply/)

Earn the degree your career longs for and get the boost you need.

#####  [Executive MBA](https://www.scu.edu/business/executive-mba/)

Designed specifically for seasoned  
managers with 10 or more years of experience.

  * [Program Overview](https://www.scu.edu/business/executive-mba/)

#####  [MS in Business Analytics]( https://www.scu.edu/business/ms-business-
analytics/)

Big data. The Internet of everything which blends business acumen with data
analysis.  


  * [Program Overview]( https://www.scu.edu/business/ms-business-analytics/)
  * [Curriculum](/business/ms-business-analytics/curriculum/ "Home » Home » Leavey School of Business » MS Business Analytics » Curriculum")

#####  [MS in Information Systems](https://www.scu.edu/business/ms-
information-systems/)

A project-based curriculum draws from the real-world that you can put to use
immediately.

  * [Program Overview](https://www.scu.edu/business/ms-information-systems/)
  * [Curriculum](https://www.scu.edu/business/ms-information-systems/curriculum/)
  * [Faculty and Staff](https://www.scu.edu/business/ms-information-systems/faculty/)

#####  [4+1 Program](https://www.scu.edu/business/ms-4plus1/)

4+1 is a program designed to provide SCU undergraduates an opportunity to
complete a master’s degree in as little as 10 months.

  * Get Started Senior Year
  * Scholarships Available
  * STEM Certified 

#####  [Evening MBA](https://www.scu.edu/business/evening-mba/)

A Part-time program that explores global business.

  * [Program Overview](https://www.scu.edu/business/evening-mba/)
  * [Curriculum](/business/evening-mba/curriculum/ "Home » Home » Leavey School of Business » Evening MBA » Curriculum")
  * [Faculty and Staff](https://www.scu.edu/business/evening-mba/faculty/)

#####  [Online MBA](https://onlinemba.scu.edu/)

Built in Silicon Valley, Online MBA offers a well-rounded, real-world
applicable curriculum.

  * [Program Overview](https://onlinemba.scu.edu/admissions/requirements)
  * [Curriculum](https://onlinemba.scu.edu/academics/curriculum)
  * [Faculty and Staff](https://onlinemba.scu.edu/academics/faculty)

#####  [MS in Financial Analytics](https://www.scu.edu/business/ms-finance/)

The core of successful innovation. If ideas don’t get funded, products don’t
get to market.

  * [Program Overview](https://www.scu.edu/business/ms-finance/)
  * [Curriculum](https://www.scu.edu/business/ms-finance/curriculum/)
  * [Faculty and Staff](https://www.scu.edu/business/ms-finance/faculty/)

#####  [Online MS in Marketing](https://onlinemba.scu.edu/academics/marketing-
masters)

Designed to accommodate and anticipate ongoing innovative change in the 21st-
century marketing environment.

  * Four 10-week quarters
  * Complete in as little as one year
  * Part-time and full-time enrollment
