## Hiring a Graduate Business Student

![](/media/leavey-school-of-business/illustrations-mp/Hiring-Grad-
Student-250x250.jpg)

Graduate Business Career Management offers employers a number of ways to
connect with students and alumni. Employers can use any of the options below
to advertise an open position or directly meet qualified candidates.

##### Job Postings for Graduate Business Students

__

Submit a job or internship posting to be distributed to Graduate Business
students and alumni. Employers also have the option to have the posting
distributed to the appropriate student networks and posted on the Graduate
Business Career Management and the Leavey School of Business Linkedin groups.
This service is FREE for all employers.

Graduate Business Career Management advises all employers to comply with the
Department of Justice regarding best practices for job postings. Please note
that job postings should be written to comply with the Immigration and
Nationality Act. For more information please review best practices as outlined
on the Department of Justice website:
<http://www.justice.gov/crt/about/osc/htm/best_practices.php>.

**[Post a job here](https://www.scu.edu/business/graduates/career-
management/employers/employer-job-posting-form/)**  »

##### Graduate Business Career Management LinkedIn Group

__

Connect directly with Graduate Business students and alumni [through the
Graduate Business Career Management Linkedin
group](https://www.linkedin.com/groups/2821079) »

Employers also have the option of announcing upcoming recruiting events and
posting job opportunities through this group.

##### Handshake

__

Reach out to all Santa Clara University students and alumni through Handshake
to post jobs or internship opportunities. [Visit Handshake
here](https://www.scu.edu/careercenter/employers/login/) »

##### On-Campus Interviewing

__

Employers can arrange to spend an entire day at Santa Clara to conduct
interviews with interested, qualified candidates for internship and full-time
positions. On-Campus Interviewing is managed by the Santa Clara University
Career Center. [Visit the Career Center website for more information on OCI
here ](https://www.scu.edu/careercenter/employers/meet/) »

##### University Career Fairs

__

Join other top employers in meeting candidates from Santa Clara University
during our quarterly career fair. [Visit the Career Center website for more
information on Career Fair dates and registration
here](https://www.scu.edu/careercenter/employers/meet/) »

##### Hiring International Students

__

Answers to commonly asked questions about hiring international students.
Overviews of the different types of visas as well the types of training,
sponsorship, and work authorization are covered on this site. [Visit the
website of our International Student Services office
here](https://www.scu.edu/globalengagement/international-students/working--
academic-internships/) »
